# Lab 1: Basic Nmap Scan

## 🎯 Objective
Understand how to scan a target and identify open ports.

## 🛠️ Tool Used
- Nmap

## 📌 Target
192.168.1.1 (or scanme.nmap.org)

## 💻 Command
nmap -sS 192.168.1.1

## 📊 Output
(Paste output or screenshot)

## 🔍 Explanation
- -sS → SYN scan (stealth scan)
- Finds open ports without full connection

## 🧠 What I Learned
- How port scanning works
- Importance of open ports in recon
